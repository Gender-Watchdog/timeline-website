Evidence #2: University of British Columbia (Canada)

The investigation into the University of British Columbia (UBC) reveals a pattern of evasion. It took a government intervention to get the truth.

Step 1: The Request

We asked for any records of communication between UBC and Dongguk for a full year (Sept 2024 - Sept 2025).

Step 2: The Evasion

UBC delayed their response, claiming a "backlog" and refusing to provide a timeline.

Step 3: The Government Intervention

We appealed to the BC Office of the Information & Privacy Commissioner (OIPC). They intervened, forcing UBC to respond.

Step 4: The Proof (Zero Records)

The Final Verdict: UBC produced zero records. No emails. No agreements. No communication for a full year. A partnership cannot exist without communication.
